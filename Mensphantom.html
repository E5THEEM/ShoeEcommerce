A NEW LEVEL OF PRECISION.

The Nike Phantom Vision Elite Dynamic Fit FG brings the fierce precision of street play to the pitch. A foot-hugging inner sleeve is concealed in a Flyknit constructed outer layer to create a boot for the finishers, the providers and the battlers of tomorrow's game.

Benefits

  • Quad-Fit mesh sleeve contours to your foot.
  • Dynamic Fit collar wraps your ankle to link your foot and lower leg for a seamless feel with snug comfort.
  • Ghost Lace system tightens with one pull and stays hidden for a clean striking surface and precision on the ball.
  • Flyknit construction with moulded micro-texture for precise touch.
  • Plate provides traction on firm-ground pitches.
  • Lightweight, Aggressive Traction
  • A super-light, full-length plate feels responsive and has multi-directional studs for quick cuts and bursts of speed.
